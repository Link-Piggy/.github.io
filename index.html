Hello Candidate

This is the page I made for the students who didn't get good grades at the national college entrance exam.

First, let me ask you a queation. What do you think is the purpose of education?

like this?

Some people think it is getting into a good university, others think it is getting a good job. All of these are true. In general, education is about developing a wide range of abilities, but passing exams is definitely not the purpose of education.

From my limited experience, when you enter the workplace, people look for your thinking skills, communication skills and teamwork.

Please don't keep getting caught up in your emotions, go and do something that engages with society, you will find a big world and lots of opportunities. There is much more to life than just the high school entrance exam.
